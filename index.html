Questions

Got questions? Good.

How much does a new roof actually cost?
Most of my asphalt replacements land between $10,000 and $22,000. It depends on the size of the roof, the pitch, the materials you pick, and whether the decking underneath needs any work. You'll get an itemized quote after I inspect. No bait and switch.
How long does the install take?
Most homes we finish in 1 to 2 days. If it's a big place, two stories and steep, or the decking needs replacing, it might be two or three.
Do you handle insurance claims?
Yes. If there's hail or storm damage, I document it, meet your adjuster on site, and handle the back and forth. Most folks pay just their deductible.
What warranty do I get?
50-year manufacturer warranty on the materials, plus a 10-year workmanship guarantee from us, in writing. If something fails in that window, I come back and fix it. No "act of God" fine print.
Are you licensed and insured?
Fully licensed in Ohio, bonded, $2M liability and workers' comp. Happy to send certificates before we start.
Do you offer financing?
Yes. We partner with a national lender that offers 0% APR promo plans and longer-term options, so homeowners are getting a new roof for as little as $99 a month. Checking your rate takes about 5 minutes and doesn't hit your credit score.
Will you pressure me during the inspection?
No. That's the whole reason Big Horn exists. Take the week. Take a month. I'll be here when you're ready.
What shingles do you install?
Standard install is a 30-year architectural asphalt shingle from a top manufacturer, backed by a 50-year material warranty. If you want to upgrade to impact-rated or designer shingles, we can walk through your options.
